Pomadora Gnocchi

Description

Pomadora Gnochhi is an exellent dish for rainy nights. This recipe is extremely tasty, easy to make, and will leave you wanting more as the night goes on. Sit back, pour a glass of wine and put on some jazz for this elegent recipie.

Ingredients

  • 18 oz Italian Gnocchi
  • 20 oz Marazona Tomatoes
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley, Oregeno, Thyme

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and add the spices.
  2. Remove the spices and add the onion and garlic
  3. Add the tomatoes, simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Boil the gnocchi
  5. Add the Gnocchi and cream sauce to the tomatoes and voila!
  6. Enjoy!
