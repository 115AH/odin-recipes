Cereal

Description

Cereal is a classic American breakfast dish. Although not complex, this morning staple will get you fueled for the day while also prividing your tastebuds with ample amounts of tasty pleasure.

Ingredients

  • Ceral
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Grab your favorite cereal
  2. Get a bowl, milk, and metal spoon
  3. Combine the ingredients in the bowl (set the spoon aside for later)
  4. Use the spoon to eat the cereal
  5. Enjoy!
